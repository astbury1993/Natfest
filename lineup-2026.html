SKA TRAIN

Ska Train celebrate the UK ska revival of the late 1970s and early 80s, a movement pioneered by Jerry Dammers’ iconic 2-Tone record label. This era brought us legendary bands like The Specials, Madness, Bad Manners, and The Selecter.

The band’s seven-piece line-up features spine-tingling horns (trombone and saxophone) and a rock-solid rhythm section, with guitar, keys, and vocals that transport audiences back to an explosive musical era of punk, politics, and messages of peace.

Since 2023, Ska Train have:

  • Established a record-breaking run of sold-out dates at Leamington’s award-winning Temperance Music Bar
  • Supported US ska-punk band Monkey on their UK tour
  • Performed live on stage with former members of The Specials
  • Headlined festivals including Congleton Jazz and Blues Festival, MurphFest, Astonbury, and Art in the Park

So if you’ve ever set sail in a Night Boat To Cairo, gazed at your Mirror In The Bathroom, or simply done Too Much Too Young, get on board the Ska Train now for an unforgettable live musical journey that will truly take you One Step Beyond...
