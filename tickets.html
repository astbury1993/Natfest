Natfest ticket sales announcement! 🎫

Tickets will be on sale on Saturday 27th September for Natfest 2026!

Here’s what’s included on the day..👇

🎶 Live music and entertainment from various artists and bands

🍔 Freshly prepared food options from several different vendors

🍻 Licensed Bar for Alcoholic and Non-alcoholic drinks

🎟️ Raffle with many chances to win a prize

🎨 Face painting/glitter and festival hair braiding

🏰 Bouncy castles for the kids

🏆 Multiple competitions to enter eg. Best dressed

🤫 Plus many more surprises to be announced along the way 🎉

Natfest is coming back bigger and better next year with new acts and many improvements to soon be announced 🗣️

All proceeds will go to LOROS hospice in memory of Natalie Leader who was an amazing wife, mum and friend.

The event will be held on the 27/06/26 11:00-23:30 in Sileby Leicestershire (Full address will be provided on your ticket)

Prices 💰

  • Adults - £30
  • Children (5-16) - £15
  • Under 5’s - FREE
Tickets will be provided nearer to the time of the event and will be exchanged at the gate for wristbands. Only those with tickets will gain entry to the event. There will not be tickets available to buy on the gate. This is an invite only event and not open to the general public. Check out our Instagram and Facebook pages for more details on how to get tickets.

We really hope you can all make it and help towards raising as much money as possible for the amazing team and LOROS.

Much love

The Natfest team
